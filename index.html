Amplitude vs Supermetrics

Two tools. Two different questions.

These are complements, not duplicates. One consolidates what the ad platforms reported. The other reconstructs what the person actually did.

Supermetrics

Marketing data pipeline & reporting
  • ✓Connects every ad platform into one place
  • ✓Cleans, blends and refreshes dashboards from live platform data
  • ✓Great for spend, clicks, impressions, platform-reported ROAS
  • –Reports what each platform claims — largely last-click
  • –Doesn't reconstruct one person's path across sessions

Amplitude

Behavioural journey & attribution
  • ✓Follows the same user across visits and channels
  • ✓First-touch, last-touch & custom multi-touch on the same data
  • ✓Shows assisted conversions — where content helped but didn't close
  • ✓Ties channels to quote & subscription events, not just clicks
  • ✓Can import ad spend to sit beside behaviour for CAC / ROAS
If Supermetrics is the tool for aggregating channel reporting, Amplitude is the tool for understanding the real customer journey behind those reports — exactly the layer MACSF is missing today.
What MACSF needs to send

The story only works if the data arrives clean.

Three ingredients make this analysis trustworthy: the conversions that matter, the campaign tags that identify each channel, and a stable identity so a return visit links to the first read.

  • Quote started → Quote submittedThe intent milestones, plus intermediate steps like article viewed and CTA clicked.
  • Subscription started → completedThe outcome the whole business is optimising for.
  • UTM + referrer on every visitSo Amplitude can tell Taboola, Outbrain and Google apart at each touch.

  • A channel classifierRoll raw tags into marketer-friendly channels: group Taboola + Outbrain as "Programmatic Content", Google as "Paid Search / SEA".

  • Content metadata (recommended)Article title, theme and landing page — to learn which content themes assist Health Insurance conversions.

The path forward

Five steps to make it real.

Track clean UTMs & referrer data

Ensure every campaign URL carries consistent tags, and the browser SDK captures referrer and gclid.

Define a simple channel classifier

Programmatic Content (Taboola + Outbrain) vs Paid Search / SEA — retroactive and query-time.

Track quote & subscription events

Plus the intermediate steps that reveal where content assists even when it doesn't close.

Optionally import ad spend

Bring impressions, clicks and cost in for CAC and ROAS in the same layer.

Analyse with attribution Data Tables

Switch models on the marketing attribution dashboard and let the assist story surface.
